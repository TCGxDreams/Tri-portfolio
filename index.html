Based in · BornHo Chi Minh City · 2008

I'm a Maths-Interdisciplinary (Toán Liên Ngành) student at PTNK - VNU-HCM High School for the Gifted. I got in as the entrance-exam valedictorian, so everyone expected me to just do math. Instead, I started filming it.

I built an educational TikTok that turns complicated formulas into 15-second videos kids actually watch. It grew to 15K+ followers and 13.4M views, 800K+ likes, ~100K hours watched. Somewhere in the comments I became the math mentor I never had. Off-screen I led media & events too: Head of Media for the Badminton Club, head organiser of a charity concert, first place in the school film contest.

It's all the same skill: making people care, fast. That's why I'm chasing Professional Communication at RMIT: I can already feel what makes a message land, now I want to learn the why and build it on purpose.
